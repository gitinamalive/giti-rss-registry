# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct.

## Our standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus on evidence and technical/editorial quality.
- Avoid harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory behavior.

## Scope

Applies to all project spaces including issues, pull requests, and discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that violates this policy and may restrict participation for repeated or severe violations.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately to project maintainers through the contact method listed in the repository README.
